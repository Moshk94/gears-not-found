PLAY

How to play

  1. Buy a tower and place it on the map.
  2. Control all towers with the mouse - towers will automatically shoot at mouse location.
  3. Click a tower in between rounds to open up upgrade menu.
  4. Towers will lock up after 40.4 seconds, preventing the tower from moving, the tower however will still shoot in the direction it's facing.

Enemies

  1. Enemies will follow the highlighted path.
  2. Enemies will take the shortest path to the end goal.
  3. If there are no valid path, the enemies will break through towers to reach the goal.
